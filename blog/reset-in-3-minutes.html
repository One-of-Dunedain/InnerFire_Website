3-Minute Reset Protocol

Mar 4, 2026 5 min read

This is the reset loop I use between heavy work blocks. It is short enough to do anywhere and structured enough to feel measurable.

Minute-by-minute sequence

Minute 1: exhale-focused breathing. Minute 2: equal inhale/exhale. Minute 3: natural breathing while keeping posture stable.

Use this as a baseline and adapt only one variable at a time: duration, count, or posture. Changing everything at once makes the protocol unreliable.
