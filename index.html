Why it actually works

  • Break the loop. Doomscrolling, overthinking, anxiety spirals - your brain is stuck in a pattern. InnerFire gives it something physical and immediate to do instead.
  • Multisensory grounding. Your breath controls fire, vibration, sound, and light at once. This sensory load activates your vagus nerve and pulls your nervous system back to calm.
  • No friction when you need it most. No paywall after 5 minutes. No sign-up gate. No guided meditation voice. Just you, your breath, and the environment. Open the app - breathe - done.

From Ukraine, with a purpose

Hi, I'm Marian Kushnir. I live in Ukraine.

When the full-scale invasion started, I, like millions of Ukrainians, needed something to cope with the stress of war, insomnia, anxiety, etc.

I decided to try breathing exercises.

Meditation apps and breathing timers feel abstract when you're under a ballistic missile attack, trust me. :)

I needed something capable of quickly shifting my brain's attention.

I built InnerFire for myself, and it helped me a lot.

Now I give it to you - find your own InnerFire...
